# Session Summary: The Miracle of Krezk and the Flesh Bride

**PCs present:** Broseph (Juan Maldonado), Erdan (Marcos), Renard / Yankee (Nacho). *Note: At the end of the session, Paloma and Álvaro intervene out of character.*
**NPCs present:** Clovin Bellevue, The Abbot, Vasilka, Ezmerelda d'Avenir, Dimitris (Burgomaster of Cres/Krezk), Yelenko (Dimitris' son), Krezk Guards, Bandits/Werewolves.
**Locations:** St. Markovia's Abbey, Cres (Krezk), Road to Valaki (Vallaki).

### Main Development and Loose Ends
The group discovers the Abbot's true and disturbing plan to save Barovia: to create a perfect flesh bride (a golem) for Strahd to fall in love with and lift the curse. After spending the night at the Abbey—where they secretly meet the bride herself (Vasilka) and the monster hunter Ezmerelda—the Abbot demonstrates his divine power by resurrecting the deceased son of Krezk's Burgomaster. However, the miracle has a price: the Burgomaster must obtain a wedding dress from Vallaki within a month, or his son will die again. The group departs as escort to Vallaki but are ambushed by werewolves on the road, leaving the session on a cliffhanger just as combat begins.

**Loose ends:**
*   Will Ezmerelda survive her solo foray into Castle Ravenloft?
*   Who is the "grumpy old man" who taught Ezmerelda to hunt, and why is he hiding?
*   Will they reach Vallaki in time to get the wedding dress and save the Burgomaster's family from the Abbot's ultimatum?
*   Is Krezk's holy pool truly magical, and will there be any side effects from the water Erdan drank?

### Session Joke
The best joke arises from a tragic story in Barovia's lore. When Ezmerelda tells them that Strahd annihilated all the women of an ancient race of elves, leaving only the men alive as punishment and forbidding them from leaving Barovia, the group quickly deduces the reproductive consequences. Amidst laughter, they conclude that "all elves are gay" by obligation, as they have been locked away for centuries without women, commenting with dark humor that they "have nothing to lose" when facing Strahd, because they are already screwed for life.

The Abbot warns them not to leave their room at night due to the mongrels roaming the Abbey.

Renard manages to gently persuade her to return to her quarters before the Abbot discovers her, promising they will get her a worthy dress for her wedding.

**Location:** Village of Krezk (Cres) and the Burgomaster's house.
**Loot:** Restorative water from the holy pool (Erdan fills his canteen).

**Summary:**
In the morning, after enduring a very drunk Clovin playing the violin, the Abbot appears and asks them to follow him. They descend from the Abbey towards the village of Krezk. The villagers flee in terror at the sight of the Abbot. They arrive at the square, where they are met by Burgomaster Dimitris surrounded by guards and armed villagers. The Abbot enters the Burgomaster's house, where they are mourning the corpse of his teenage son, Yelenko. With a prayer to Lathander, the Abbot resurrects the boy, causing shock and joy. However, the Abbot collects his favor: he demands that the Burgomaster obtain a high-quality wedding dress from Vallaki within a month. If he fails, the son will die again, and the Burgomaster will pay with his life. The Burgomaster asks the adventurers to escort his wife by carriage to Vallaki to buy the dress. Before departing, Erdan visits the village's holy pool and discovers that its water has restorative properties, filling his canteen.
