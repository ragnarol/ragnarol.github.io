# Thirst on Hilfer and the Trail of Ergo

**PCs present:** Den, Pika, Klaiton, Lyra, Yaang-kee, Daijiro.
**NPCs present:** O'Leary, Ealing, Informant pilot, Local beggars, Registry bureaucrat, Local gunners.

**Main character and campaign developments:**
The group discovers that the Kingdom of Drinax's reputation on Hilfer is abysmal due to past bombardments that destroyed the planet's water infrastructure. Upon learning that a local pirate, Miria Silverhand, is raiding ice harvesters and causing a humanitarian crisis due to lack of water, the group decides to intervene. Their goal is twofold: hunt the pirate to seize her ship (a 400-ton vessel equipped with a crane, ideal for their future expedition) and return the stolen water to the planet, presenting themselves as envoys of Drinax to clear the Kingdom's name.

**Loose ends:**
*   Why did the freighter *Garland* bring a massive shipment of food from a red, radioactive world like Ergo 13 years ago?
*   What cargo and which passenger did an engineer take from Tech-World to the gas giant of Ergo in a local system ship?
*   The relentless Captain Jagad of the Third Imperium, now in command of the *Comino*, is following the trail of the characters' former corsair ship (*Águila de Nom* / *Banshee*) and has headed toward Goertel.

**The Session Joke:**
"The Hacker without his ChatGPT." During a Computers roll to access the spaceport archives, Pika tried to help Yaang-kee. However, they realized that Pika had lost his high-tech intrusion software (level 12) in the old mission against the Ihatei. Having become accustomed to the program doing everything for him "as if it were ChatGPT," Pika’s help was a disaster and actively "un-helped" the group, nearly triggering the government system's alarms if not for a correction to the roll.
